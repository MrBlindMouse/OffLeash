About Offleash


OffLeash is a website built with the goal of creating communities around dogs(or any freedom loving pets).

Find pet friendly parks or trails, chat with the local community, and get shoutouts from local pet friendly businesses.

Registered members are encouraged to post reviews on the venues and businesses, as well as add new or missing venues to the map
